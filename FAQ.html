FAQ

1. What is the eligibility criteria for the Army Entry Exam?

The applicant must be a citizen, aged between 18-25 years, and should have at least a high school diploma or equivalent.

2. How do I apply for the exam?

You can apply by filling out the application form on our website.

3. What documents are required for the application?

You will need a valid ID proof, educational certificates, and a recent photograph.

4. Is there an application fee?

No, the application process is completely free of charge.

5. How will I be notified about the exam date?

You will receive an email with the exam date, venue, and further instructions after your application is processed.

6. Can I make changes to my application after submission?

Yes, you can contact our support team at support@armyexam.com to request changes.

7. What is the syllabus for the exam?

The exam will cover topics like general knowledge, aptitude, and basic fitness assessment. Detailed syllabus will be sent to your registered email.

8. How can I contact support?

You can reach out to us at support@armyexam.com or call us at +1-800-123-4567.
